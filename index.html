Project-based Learning in Computer Science

Integrating Computer Science Projects into your classroom

By 2020 there will be approximately 1.4 million computer science (CS) related jobs in the US alone with an expected shortfall of 75% qualified candidates through traditional CS college programs and boot camps. This is only one of several statistics that show that we are quickly moving to a world where CS skills are the highest in demand.

In spite of the emerging need for CS skills, opportunities for CS training remain elusive for many students. Furthermore, most of this training still happens in the post-secondary and continuing education world. As the push to begin CS instruction in K-12 grows, it is imperative that educators be prepared to deliver effective and applicable instruction based on real-life skills. We have crafted this course to support teachers in the development and implementation of a project-based approach to teaching Computer Science (CS).
